Our Story

Founded in 2020, Hami MiniMarket began as a small neighborhood grocery store with a simple mission: to provide fresh, high-quality produce at affordable prices. Today, we've grown into a trusted online marketplace while staying true to our roots.

We work directly with local farmers to bring you the freshest fruits and vegetables, supporting sustainable agriculture and reducing our carbon footprint.
